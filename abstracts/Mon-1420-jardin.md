# Neutron Calibrations: Dosimetry and Personnel Protection

**Daniel Jardin, Alan Thompson**

- National Institute of Standards and Technology, Gaithersburg MD 20899

---

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) is responsible for providing calibration services to industries that require traceability to a national standard. Some of those industries (e.g., nuclear energy production, defense, homeland security, and medicine) have a common thread of protecting personnel that work in the vicinity of ionizing radiation hazards. This presentation is focused on the calibration of measurement devices that are specifically designed for use in neutron fields at radiation- protection levels. I will describe the facility where these device calibrations are performed, walk through the procedure for a typical calibration, and highlight some external collaborations that are also utilizing this facility. There will also be brief discussions on two other neutron calibration services provided by NIST.

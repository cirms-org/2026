# Three-dimensional dose and dose rate measurement of proton FLASH beams using Multilayer Strip Ionization Chamber (MLSIC)

**Shuang Zhou** <br>
*TetraImaging LLC*

**Tiezhi Zhang** <br>
*Stony Brook University*

Proton is the only FLASH-RT radiation treatment modality that can treat deeply seated targets at this time. A quality assurance tool for both dose and dose rate distributions is needed for future clinical proton FLASH-RT treatment. Point measurement of the average dose rate is insufficient, as the average dose rate distribution of a scanning proton beam is highly heterogeneous. We will describe an MLSIC device that can measure and reconstruct 3D dose and dose-rate distributions for proton FLASH-RT beams. The principle and measurement results of clinical proton FLASH beams will be presented.
